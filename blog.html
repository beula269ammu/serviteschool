SCHOOL RULES



  • All the students are required to reach school in time and attend the morning assembly. If a student reports late to school (after 9:00a.m.) then he / she will be marked absent.

  • No student can leave the school during school hours for any reason. No written request in this regard will be entertained from parent / guardian.

  • Students are strictly forbidden to use vulgar/filthy language while talking to one another. Students indulging in truancy and bullying will be liable to suspension or expulsion from the school.

  • Cleanliness is expected from all the students. Their nails must be properly clipped, teeth must be cleaned and hair neatly trimmed and combed.

  • Office bearers STUDENTS COUNCIL should wear their respective badges daily.

  • No student shall criticize any member of the staff, or behave rudely with him/her.

  • Any student indulging in misbehavior with staff and fellow students shall be liable for disciplinary action/rustication from the school.

  • Student must deposit with the office any article or money found by them in the school premises.

  • Students must treat school property with care. Vandalism is a serious offence. Students and their parents will be held financially responsible for any damage caused to the school property.

  • Students are not allowed to bring with them any magazine or papers other than the library /prescribed books. Any obscene book / magazine found with the child will invite disciplinary action.

  • Lending or borrowing money is strictly forbidden.

  • The school is not responsible for the loss of money, gold ornaments or any other valuable articles, It is therefore advised not be bring or wear valuable articles to school.

  • Students are not allowed to use the school telephone. Also, they shall not be called to answer any telephone call except in an emergency.

  • The school reserves the right to suspend/rusticate a student whose conduct has an adverse influence on other students.

  • Distributions of chocolates / cakes / eatables / gifts are not allowed on Birthdays.

  • Possession of any electronic gadgets / ornaments is strictly prohibited. Violation of this code will lead to stringent disciplinary action –to extent of confiscation of the article / suspension of the student.

  • Students should always report in School Uniform only

Servite CBSE School is run by the sisters of the Congregation of Mother of Sorrows Servants of Mary, known as ‘Servites’.
